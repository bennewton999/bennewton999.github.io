From my blog over at blulyne.com/blog. Here is a video to show the basic function of webtocatalog.com.  In the video I use a tab delimited text file to create the catalog that I created from the Amazon API.  The file has the item, description, price, image url, category, sub category and manufacturer.  Even though I use this file, you can use a file generated for your Amazon store, Google Product Feed, Pricegrabber, or any other tab delimited file or excel sheet with at least the above fields.
