I am on Plaxo checking out the new features.  I really was hoping to find a Facebook killer, but I have to say, so far all I've found is a mini feed clone.  It does have a great interface, but after a few minutes you can see how the open platform is benefiting Facebook and until there is some more options to choose from, Plaxo should just keep doing what they do best, and that's syncing your contacts and calendars. Once you've added your friends, you're done.  You wait for them to update their YouTube videos, Blog, Twitter or Pownce feed.  I imagine when they add events to their calendar, they may show in your feed too, but I haven't been that deep yet.

You can differentiate between friends, family and business contacts which is a huge plus that everyone has been asking for on Facebook.  But what good it does on Plaxo as it stands now remains to be seen.

I'm sure these guys aren't done yet though.  I am sure this is just the beginning.  They probably have more up their sleeve.  They just better hurry or it will be too late to matter.
