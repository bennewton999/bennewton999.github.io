Well, I have made my 3rd switch of blogging platforms.  I started with  Typepad.com about two and a half years ago.  It was good, but not worth the $$ for a personal blog written for fun and not for profit.  I liked the simplicity at the time, but was looking for something with more potential for customization. So I switched to Community Server about a year later.  I moved Valor's Forums and Blogs to Community Server also, which I still use for them, but for my personal blog, I never was comfortable with it.  So much so, I basically stopped posting.  When I did post, I could never get Technorati.com pings to work etc.  It has worked great for Valor's Blog, but I may move that soon too.

And now, I'm using Wordpress.com.  I have to say, so far I am very happy.  They have definitely made some changes since I last looked at it.  I never realized how many of the blogs I read are Wordpress blogs.  It's a great site.  Simple and clean.  As you can see, I am using the default theme and I don;t see any reason to change it.  I will put up a custom header at some point, but this will work for now.

So subscribe to my feed if you're interested.  I have some interesting projects I am working on and will begin to introduce them here in the near future.  And I will start spewing useless and not so useless info here almost daily now.
