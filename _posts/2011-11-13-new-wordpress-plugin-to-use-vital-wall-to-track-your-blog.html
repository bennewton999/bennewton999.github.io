I have released a new wordpress plugin today that you can use to set up a wordpress blog to work with one of your Vital Walls.  All you need to do is set up a wall and then download the plugin and install it on your blog, add the wall id in the options and your wall will begin showing blog titles live when they are visited.

Download and Install Plugin

You can download the plugin at:

http://vitalwall.com/wp_plugin/vitalwall_wp.zip

Now, unzip the file into the /wp-content/plugins/ directory of your blog.  Then go to the plugins page of your admin panel and activate the plugin.  Once activated, you can go to the Vital Wall options under the Settings menu and enter your wall id.

That's it!  When someone visits a post on your blog, the title will show up on your wall.
