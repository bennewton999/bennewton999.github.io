When you walk into a store and you see a crowd around a shelf, you're going to go see what they are looking at.  You want the same thing to happen when people visit your website?  Then put a vital wall on your front page that shows a live feed of currently viewed items.  This will enable vistors to see what is being viewed live.  If there is a hot item, people entering your store are going to see it.

Take a look at the front page of silverbullet.com.  You will see a "Hot Items" section on the right.  This shows all the items being viewed, live!

It's quick an easy to install.

Quick Install

First, set up a wall in your account.

Then, go to your product page in your site.  At the bottom of the page, insert the following code.

Source code    
<script type="text/javascript">

var vitaldata='<div><div id="manufacturer"><%=rs("manufacturer_short_name")%></div><a href="LINK_TO_ITEM"><img src="LINK_TO_IMAGE"></a></div>';

var wall_id='YOUR_WALL_ID';

</script>

<script type="text/javascript" src="http://www.vitalwall.com/vitaldata.js"></script>

Of course, you can pass whatever you like in the vitaldata variable. This is just a format I used when writing silverbullet.com. Now, when your product page is visited, the data for that item is passed to your vitalwall and is available for your wall.

Include Your Wall

To include the wall on your front page, insert the following code in your page. Put the div with the id of results where you want your wall.

Source code    
<div id="results"></div>

Then, towards the bottom of the page, insert the following code.

Source code    
<script type="text/javascript">
var wall_id='YOUR_WALL_ID';
var div_id='results';
</script>
<script type="text/javascript" src="http://vitalwall.com/include_wall.js"></script>

Use CSS to customize your wall

You can use CSS to customize the look of your wall. Add borders, colors, margins etc.

That's it. You now have a live view of what is being viewed in your ecommerce store, and better yet, so do your customers.
